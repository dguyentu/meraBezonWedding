Our Story

Every Relationship Has A Beginning

It’s not often that two people from seemingly opposite ends of the world find one another at the perfect time and fall in love. However, such is the case for our medical school sweethearts. For Mira, who grew up in New York and came to California in pursuit of her dream to become a physician, the move across the country wasn’t easy. And for Bezon, staying in California for another four years of medical school was all he ever wanted. Or so he thought, because little did our two love birds know the journey they would embark on together. First friendship turned into affection, then affection turned into love, and eventually love turned into lifelong commitment. On February 19th, 2023, this commitment was made official at Torrey Pines in San Diego, California. And on July 21st, 2024 in Glendale, California, this commitment will be celebrated with all the beloved friends and family of the soon-to-be husband and wife. Please join us in celebrating this joyous occasion, as we bear witness to the beginning of a lifetime of love and happiness for Mira Bishawi and Bezon Rustomi. Thank you and we look forward to hopefully seeing you all there!
